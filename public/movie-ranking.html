The Best Movies According to Precious

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Flowers in the Attic

You won't believe a mother locked up her children in the attic for several years just to inherit her Father's wealth. In an attempt to get rid of them, she adds a portion of rat poison to their meal. the story is based on a novel which I think is super interesting to read.

Miss Bridgerton

A group of young girls must keep themselves chaste and find suitors at the age of 18 because of what society expects from them. I totally love the whole British accent used in the movie.

Wednesday

This mystery girl is the key to all the secrets at her school. She's cold, icy and not scared of anything.
